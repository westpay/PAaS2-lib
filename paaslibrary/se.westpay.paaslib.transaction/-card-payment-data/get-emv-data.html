fun getEmvData(): EmvData

Get EMV data.

Return

EMV data from the card. This is applies for cards that have EMV data:

  • Cards inserted and read in the chip reader.

  • Contactless cards that are tapped to the terminal.

Note! There are contactless cards that does not contain EMV data, but they are not common in Europe. They are more common in North America (which means tourists might have them).
